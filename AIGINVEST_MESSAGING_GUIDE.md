# AIGINVEST Brand Messaging Guide

**For:** All teams, marketing, documentation, communication  
**Purpose:** Consistent positioning of AIGINVEST, Diana, AIOS, and North Star ONE  
**Updated:** July 6, 2026  

---

## Quick Reference

| Term | What It Is | Use When | DON'T Use For |
|------|-----------|----------|---------------|
| **AIGINVEST** | Company, platform, ecosystem | Customer destination, main brand | Technology, specific features |
| **Diana** | AI companion | Interface, assistant, primary way users interact | Operating system, hardware, platform |
| **AIOS** | Operating system | Technology, developer documentation | Customer messaging, main brand |
| **North Star ONE** | Hardware device (phone) | Hardware-specific features, device-level specs | Platform, ecosystem, general messaging |

---

## Customer-Facing Messaging

### Level 1: Discovery

**Headline:**
> Discover AIGINVEST — Where your AI companion meets your productivity

**What we're saying:**
- AIGINVEST is the destination
- Diana is the companion
- It's about productivity and usefulness

**NOT:**
- "Try North Star ONE" (hardware first)
- "AIOS lets you..." (too technical)
- "A phone for..." (wrong category)

---

### Level 2: Onboarding

**Headline:**
> Meet Diana. One AI companion. Every device.

**Subheading:**
> Sign in once. Use Diana everywhere. On web, desktop, phone, or tablet.

**What we're saying:**
- Diana is universal
- One account works everywhere
- Multiple devices supported
- Diana is the interface

**NOT:**
- "North Star ONE is your AI phone"
- "AIOS makes everything smarter"
- "Hardware required"

---

### Level 3: Deepening

**Headline:**
> Part of AIGINVEST

**Subheading:**
> One account. Diana. Every device. Every service.

**Features:**
- Use Diana on web
- Premium experience on North Star ONE (optional)
- Sync everything across devices
- Scale as you grow

**What we're saying:**
- Platform is primary
- Hardware is premium option
- Ecosystem scales together
- AIGINVEST is the relationship

## When Each Term Is Appropriate

### Use "AIGINVEST"
- Describing the company
- Describing the platform/ecosystem
- Describing customer journey
- Marketing content
- Customer communications
- "AIGINVEST users"

### Use "Diana"
- Describing the AI companion/interface
- User-facing features
- Customer communications
- "Chat with Diana"
- "Diana can help you with..."

### Use "AIOS"
- Technical documentation
- Developer documentation
- OS-specific features
- Architecture discussions
- "AIOS runs on..."

### Use "North Star ONE"
- Hardware specifications
- Device-specific features
- Hardware availability/pricing
- Hardware reviews
- "On North Star ONE, you get..."

---

## Anti-Patterns (Things We Never Say)

### Never
- "AIOS is our ecosystem" ❌
- "North Star ONE users" → "AIGINVEST users with North Star ONE" ✅
- "Diana is a phone assistant" ❌ → "Diana is your AI companion" ✅
- "AIGINVEST requires hardware" ❌ → "Premium experience on North Star ONE" ✅
- "Exclusive to North Star ONE" ❌ → "Available everywhere, premium on North Star ONE" ✅
- "Diana is part of AIOS" ❌ → "Diana works with AIOS" ✅

---

## Decision Tree

**When choosing a term, ask:**

1. Am I talking about the company/destination? → AIGINVEST
2. Am I talking about the AI interface? → Diana
3. Am I talking about the operating system? → AIOS
4. Am I talking about a specific hardware device? → North Star ONE

If you can't answer clearly, default to AIGINVEST.

---

## Review Checklist

Before publishing anything, ask:

- [ ] Does this position AIGINVEST as the destination?
- [ ] Does this make Diana the primary interface?
- [ ] Does this position North Star ONE as premium, not required?
- [ ] Does this use terminology consistently?
- [ ] Would a new user understand the hierarchy?
- [ ] Could this confuse hardware with platform?
