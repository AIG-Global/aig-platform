## How to Evaluate Every Feature Going Forward

### The Four-Question Framework

Before building anything, ask:

1. **Does it strengthen AIGINVEST?**
   - Not just North Star ONE
   - Not just Diana
   - AIGINVEST as a whole

2. **Does it make Diana more helpful?**
   - Add capability?
   - Improve accuracy?
   - Integrate new service?

3. **Does it improve North Star ONE experience?**
   - Hardware-specific benefit?
   - Unique to the device?
   - Showcase capability?

4. **Does it fit long-term AIOS architecture?**
   - Foundational?
   - Scalable?
   - Multi-device ready?

**If all four: YES → Build**  
**If any NO: → Decline or redesign**
