## Executive Summary

North Star ONE Phase 1 requires a **core team of 8-12 people** organized in 4 functional areas. The Engineering Lead joins Week 1 and immediately begins hiring 4-5 technical team members over Weeks 1-4.

**Current Status:**
- ✅ Engineering Lead: recruiting (target: Week 1)
- ⏳ Backend: 2 engineers (target: Week 2-3)
- ⏳ Frontend: 2 engineers (target: Week 2-3)
- ⏳ DevOps: 1 engineer (target: Week 2)
- ⏳ Designer: 1 person (target: Week 3)
- ⏳ Product Manager: 1 person (target: Week 1)
- ⏳ QA/Testing: 1 person (target: Week 3)

## Hiring Questions to Ask

### Technical Interviews

**For all engineers:**
1. "Tell me about the most complex system you've designed."
2. "How do you approach performance optimization?"
3. "Tell me about a time you had to make a tough architectural decision."
4. "What's your approach to testing and quality?"
5. "How do you handle technical debt?"

**For backend:**
1. "How do you design APIs for scalability?"
2. "Tell me about database optimization techniques you've used."
3. "How would you architect a real-time system?"

**For frontend:**
1. "How do you manage state in complex React apps?"
2. "Tell me about your approach to performance optimization in React."
3. "How do you ensure accessibility in your components?"

### Culture/Fit Interviews

1. "Why are you interested in Diana?"
2. "Tell me about your experience in early-stage startups."
3. "How do you handle ambiguity and change?"
4. "What's your ideal work environment?"
5. "How do you learn and stay current?"

## Core Hiring Principles

1. **Hire for attitude, train for skill**
   - Raw talent and learning mindset > perfect resume

2. **Hire for culture fit but not culture clone**
   - We value diversity of thought and background

3. **Move fast but don't compromise quality**
   - Better to take 2 weeks to hire right than hire fast and fire fast

4. **Pay competitively from day one**
   - We want winners, not desperate people

5. **Equity vests fairly**
   - Standard 4-year vest with 1-year cliff (industry standard)

6. **Build for 10 years, not next quarter**
   - Hire people who want to stay, grow, and build something lasting
