## How Decisions Will Be Made From Now On

### The Four-Question Framework

Every feature proposal answers:

1. **Does it strengthen AIGINVEST?**
2. **Does it make Diana more helpful?**
3. **Does it improve North Star ONE experience?**
4. **Does it fit long-term AIOS architecture?**

**If all four: YES** → Add to roadmap  
**If any: NO** → Decline or redesign

---

## Weekly Review Ritual

Every Friday, we ask 5 questions:

1. Is Diana more helpful?
2. Is the UI simpler?
3. Is the platform faster?
4. Is the architecture cleaner?
5. Would we confidently demo this?

**If all YES:** On track  
**If any NO:** Identify what to fix
