## 💡 Key Insights

### On Architecture
The power isn't in the individual registries. It's in the **ecosystem of registries** working together to create a **self-describing system** that Diana can orchestrate with **natural language**.

### On Diana
Diana isn't a chat assistant. Diana is **the intelligent runtime**. Every capability is available to her through natural conversation.

### On Scalability
Traditional platforms hardcode knowledge. North Star ONE **discovers everything at runtime**. New apps, services, capabilities? Automatically available to Diana.

### On Operations
Instead of logging into multiple dashboards, **one unified dashboard** gives complete visibility into the entire platform — every service, every app, every metric, every event.
