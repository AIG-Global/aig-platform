About this data
Crypto prices and 30-day history come live from CoinGecko's public API — no key needed. Stock/index data and fundamentals require your own free API key (Alpha Vantage or Finnhub), stored only in this browser. Headlines come from Google News via a public RSS proxy and may be unavailable if that proxy is rate-limited. Grades and correlation are computed directly from that data. AI summaries (optional, your own Anthropic key) describe the numbers already on screen in plain language — they don't pull in outside information or judgment of their own. Nothing here is investment advice.
AIG Investor Alerts is a research and information tool for professionals and informed investors. Nothing here — price data, correlation, grades, news, or AI-generated summaries — is investment advice or a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold anything. Investing always carries risk of loss, in stocks, crypto, and commodities alike, regardless of whether AI is involved in the analysis. Past correlation and past performance are not predictive of future results. Data may be delayed, incomplete, or temporarily unavailable depending on third-party API limits. Consider speaking with a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.

Stock & index data

Stock and index quotes require a free API key from a provider that supports it — this app never sends your key anywhere except directly to that provider, and stores it only in your browser's local storage.

Get a free key from Alpha Vantage (25 requests/day) or Finnhub (60/min), then paste it below.

AI-generated summaries

This optional feature sends the data already shown on screen (prices, correlation values, grades) to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a plain-language summary. Your API key is sent directly to Anthropic from your browser and stored only in this browser's local storage — this app and its author never see it.

Get a key from the Anthropic Console, then paste it below. Leave it unset and the rest of the app works exactly the same, minus the AI summaries.

⚠ Before you use this tool

AIG Investor Alerts exists to surface real market data, real correlation analysis, and real news context for people who are already comfortable making their own investment decisions. Please read this before continuing:

  • Not advice.Nothing in this tool — price data, correlation, grades, or AI-generated summaries — is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold anything.
  • Risk is unavoidable.Every asset class shown here — stocks, crypto, commodities — carries real risk of loss. AI-assisted analysis does not reduce or remove that risk.
  • Past data isn't predictive.Correlation, grades, and trends shown here describe what has already happened. They do not predict what will happen next.
  • AI can be wrong.AI-generated summaries (when enabled) are a plain-language read of the data already shown, generated by a third-party model. They can misinterpret data and should be checked against the numbers themselves.
  • You decide.Any investment decision, and its outcome, is yours alone. Consider speaking with a licensed financial advisor before acting on anything you see here.
