Recording other people requires their consent — check the law where you are. Many places legally require telling everyone on a call that it's being recorded, and some require everyone's explicit consent, not just yours. This app records your own screen/microphone with your own browser permission each time — it does not connect to WhatsApp, Zoom, Teams, or any other app directly, and can't bypass or hide the recording from other participants for you.

Connect your OpenAI API key

Transcription uses OpenAI's Whisper model — Claude's API doesn't do speech-to-text, so this app uses a different provider than the rest of the AIG suite. This is separate from any Anthropic key you may already have. Get one at platform.openai.com. Pay-as-you-go, roughly $0.006/minute of audio.
