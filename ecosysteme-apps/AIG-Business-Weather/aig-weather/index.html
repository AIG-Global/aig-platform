Data sources
Regular weather: Open-Meteo (aggregates NOAA GFS, ECMWF, DWD, JMA and other national models) — no key, CC BY 4.0.
Earthquakes: USGS Earthquake Hazards Program — real-time GeoJSON feeds. Tsunami risk shown here is USGS's own flag on qualifying events (M≥~7, shallow, offshore) — for official tsunami bulletins, see tsunami.gov (Pacific/Caribbean/Atlantic) or your regional warning center directly.
Cyclones, floods, volcanoes, wildfires: GDACS (UN/EU Joint Research Centre).
Severe weather alerts (US locations only): NOAA / National Weather Service.
