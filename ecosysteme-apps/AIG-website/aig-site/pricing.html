Questions

What people ask before signing up.

Is the account balance an investment?

No. It's store credit, the same as a gift card balance. It earns no interest or yield and is not a security. You can convert it back to your bank account 1:1 at any time.

Can I earn money by referring other members?

You can earn a one-time, flat €20 credit per successful referral. There is no multi-level structure, no recruitment commission, and no way to earn from a "downline."

What happens to my balance if I cancel?

It stays yours. Convert it to your bank account or keep it for a future booking — either way, at any time.

How does WDM marketplace pricing work?

Each listing shows its price up front. You pay that price from your account balance or a card, with no markup tied to membership tier.
